How it works

Simple, fair, and designed for public transparency.

1
Choose a competition
Select Superbowl, GAA Football Final, or Hurling Final.
2
Buy squares
Enter quantity. Squares are assigned randomly.
3
Boards roll over
When a board sells out, it’s archived and a new board opens.
4
Sold-out boards only
Only fully sold boards are valid. Non-full boards near kickoff are refunded.
